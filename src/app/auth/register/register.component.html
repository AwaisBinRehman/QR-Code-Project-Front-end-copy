REGISTER FOR RIGHT BRAND ASIA FOR PRODUCTS

Register with RIGHT BRAND ASIA to get RBA barcode number for your products on renewal based subscription. The barcode /QR code subscription fee is based on the company's turnover, the number of barcodes/QR codes, & the subscription period. When choosing the number of barcodes to register, consider your current & future requirements for barcodes. Your barcode subscription includes access to our data cart service (base version only) which allows you to allocate barcode numbers, link / relink QR codes and manage all your product data.
